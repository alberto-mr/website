I am an Assistant Professor (RTDb) at the Department of Control and Computer Engineering, Politecnico di Torino, with the e-Lite Research Group. My current research targets the end user personalization of smart devices, online services, and user's behaviors with these technologies. On the one hand, I focus on end-user personalization of Internet of Things ecosystems. On the other hand, I explore how to support users to achieve what Google called the Digital Wellbeing.
